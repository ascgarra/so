Business Proposal


Shin Ha-Ri, a single woman, accepts a blind date from Jin Young-Seo, the daughter of a chaebol family, and is dumbfounded by her date. Meanwhile, Kang Tae-Moo's grandfather sets up blind dates for him, and he decides to marry the woman he meets on one of those dates.

Kim Se-Jeong

Kim Min-Gue

Kim Min Gue is a South Korean actor and model signed to Happy Tribe Entertainment. In 2013, he made his acting debut in a supporting role in the Mnet television series "Monstar." The 2019 historical series "Queen: Love and War" helped Kim become well-known for his first major leading role.
